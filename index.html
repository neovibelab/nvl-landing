About

Woojin Cha — Principal

Woojin Cha is an entertainment and culture critic, industry analyst, and the principal of Neo Vibe Lab. With 25+ years of experience spanning music, media, IP, and tech, he advises leading companies across Korea's entertainment ecosystem.

His work sits at the intersection of critical perspective and business strategy — analyzing how culture is produced, distributed, consumed, and monetized in the age of platforms and fandom.

He has been interviewed by Bloomberg, The New York Times, Le Monde, and NHK as a leading voice on the K-culture industry.

Author of Selling Perspectives (관점을 파는 일, 2025) and The Business of Hearts (마음의 비즈니스, 2023). Advisor to South Korea's Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism (2025–).

Creator and producer of K-Pop Generation (Tving documentary), SM Entertainment brand book, and other cross-format culture content.
